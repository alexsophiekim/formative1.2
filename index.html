WELCOME


Tandoori Nights has provided quality authentic Indian cuisine in the Western suburbs since 2009. We use only the finest and freshest ingredients to create traditional Indian favourites, and tailor each mouth watering meal to suit all taste buds. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff provide excellent service and you will notice our prices are also very reasonable! Our takeaway menu is available for pick up and delivery within our local suburbs, so please enquire with one of our team members when you place an order.
